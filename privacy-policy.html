Privacy Policy for MavDroid

MavDroid is provided by SK-Delta Works.

MavDroid is an Android app that provides MAVLink communication bridging and RTSP video streaming for flight controllers connected via USB, including ArduPilot and PX4.

Data Handling

MavDroid does not require user account registration.

MavDroid does not sell user data.

MavDroid does not display third-party ads.

MavDroid may use USB connection, network access, and camera access only for the core functionality of MAVLink bridging and RTSP streaming.

Unless otherwise stated, data processed by the app is handled locally on the device.
